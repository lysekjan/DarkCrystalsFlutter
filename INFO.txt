- Ravik: projektil; sending 2s, cooldown 10s, damage 5
- Brann: projektil; sending 2s, cooldown 10s, damage 5
- Nyxra: blesk do 1 cile; sending 1s, cooldown 2s, damage 30 (prvni mod prevzat z puvodni Veyra lightning)
